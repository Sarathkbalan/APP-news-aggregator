Moushumi Chatterjee On Reuniting With Amitabh Bachchan In Piku: 'The Less Said, The Better'

Published By : Shrishti Negi Trending Desk Last Updated: January 14, 2025, 13:43 IST

Veteran actress Moushumi Chatterjee worked with the legendary Amitabh Bachchan in several films. They were immensely cherished for their on-screen chemistry. However, after working on three films, including Benaam (1974), Roti Kapda Aur Makaan (1974) and Manzil (1979), they didn’t collaborate for a much longer time. And finally, in the 2015 film Piku, the on-screen duo was found sharing a screen. We stumbled upon a throwback interview with the actress, wherein she revealed her experience of reuniting with Big B after almost four decades. In an old interview, Moushumi Chatterjee was asked about her reunion with Benaam co-star Amitabh Bachchan. In reply, the veteran actress revealed that it was only because of Shoojit Sircar that they could once again share a screen. Moushumi said to Lehren Retro, “Piku, it may not….it happened only because of Soorjit-the director. He had a zid ki mujhe yeh jori ko ek aur baar present karna hai (He was adamant about casting the on-screen couple). He told in front of others."
